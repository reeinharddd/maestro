maestro

Four baton orientations. Same tool, different attitude. gold palettev0

The idea: a baton is the universal symbol of orchestration — a tool that doesn't play music itself but enables everyone else to play in sync. The angle changes the emotional stance: horizontal is calm authority, ascending is rising action, vertical is commanding presence, diagonal is decisive impact.
